Roomy (Senior Project)

Roomy

Description

This is my senior project, a social media app for incoming college students, called Roomy. This app allows incoming students to select which schools they are planning on applying to and the app will reccommend other students who have at least one common school listed. Students can add friends and message them in the app.

If you wish to learn more, you can check out my presentation or email me using my contact infromation.
